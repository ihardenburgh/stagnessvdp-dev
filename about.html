Who We Are

The Society of St. Vincent de Paul is a global Catholic lay organization that seeks to relieve the suffering of our neighbors in need. The St. Agnes Conference brings help to our Rockville Centre neighbors through person-to-person encounters.

What We Do

Vincentian volunteers make home visits to individuals or families who ask for help to gain an understanding of their unique needs. For example, here in Rockville Centre, there are neighbors facing eviction from their homes, some that may have difficulty providing food for their families, while others are homebound and experience loneliness. The Society may offer financial, material or spiritual aid. We may also may connect someone in need with outside resources for additional support.

Do You Need Help?

Please contact the St. Agnes Cathedral Rectory at (516) 766-0205 or email us at contact@stagnessvdp.org if you need help. A member of the Society will schedule a visit with you to gain an understanding of your needs.

Would You Like To Donate?

Here in Rockville Centre, the needs of our neighbors exceed our financial resources. Donating to the Society of St. Vincent de Paul – St. Agnes Conference is a great way to make a difference by helping local families. Every single dollar of your donation goes straight to helping our neighbors. If you would like to make a donation, please email us at contact@stagnessvdp.org, or contact the St. Agnes Cathedral Rectory at (516) 766-0205.

Would You Like To Volunteer?

Jesus told us that when we feed the hungry and shelter the homeless, we feed and shelter him. Volunteering as a Vincentian is a great way of responding to this Gospel call. The needs here in Rockville Centre are great and you can make a difference in the lives of our neighbors that need help. If you would like to become a member of the St. Agnes Conference, please email us at contact@stagnessvdp.org or contact the St. Agnes Cathedral Rectory at (516) 766-0205.

Contact Us

If you are a Rockville Centre resident and need help, would like to donate or would like to become a member of the Society of St. Vincent de Paul – St. Agnes Conference, please email us at contact@stagnessvdp.org or contact the St. Agnes Cathedral Rectory at (516) 766-0205.
